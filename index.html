London Tube AI

London Tube AI, also known as AI Tube Navigator, is an independent London Underground journey planning app. It provides route planning, nearby station information, live service status, disruption context, and travel insights using publicly available transport data and app-based features.

London Tube AI is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Transport for London, the Mayor of London, or any transport operator.

Data and Privacy

We aim to be transparent about how data is used. Location is used only for app functionality such as nearby stations, walking distance, disruption context, and weather/location-based features. Analytics, crash reports, performance data, and device or app identifiers are used only if enabled by the user where applicable.

For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
