Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to London Tube AI, also known as AI Tube Navigator.

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Summary: London Tube AI uses your data only to provide app functionality, improve app reliability, process purchases, and understand app performance where permitted. We do not sell your personal data.

1. App Identity

London Tube AI is an independent third-party journey planning app for London transport users. The app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Transport for London, the Mayor of London, or any transport operator.

2. Data We Collect

The app may collect or process the following types of data, depending on how you use the app and your selected settings.

Location Data

The app may collect approximate and precise location data if you grant location permission. Location data is used for app functionality, including:

  • Showing nearby Tube or transport stations
  • Calculating walking distance to nearby stations
  • Providing location-based disruption context
  • Providing weather or local travel context
  • Improving route and nearby station features while the app is in use

Location access is optional. You can deny location permission and still use other app features. You can change location permission at any time in your device settings.

App Activity

If analytics is enabled, the app may collect app interaction data, such as:

  • Screen views
  • Feature usage
  • Route planning events
  • Premium screen interactions
  • General app usage events

This data is used for analytics and app improvement.

App Information and Performance

If enabled, the app may collect crash logs, diagnostics, and other app performance data. This may include information about app crashes, errors, device state related to crashes, performance issues, and reliability metrics.

This data is used to diagnose problems, improve stability, and fix bugs.

Device or Other IDs

The app or integrated services may use device or app instance identifiers for analytics, diagnostics, crash reporting, fraud prevention, and app performance measurement.

Financial Information

If you purchase premium features, subscription or purchase history may be processed through Google Play Billing. Payment card details are handled by Google Play and are not directly collected by London Tube AI.

3. Data We Share

The app may share location data with third-party services only for app functionality, such as retrieving nearby transport information, location-based travel information, or weather/contextual data.

For example, location coordinates may be sent to transport or weather API services to return nearby station, travel, or weather information.

We do not sell your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Billing for subscriptions and purchases
  • Firebase Analytics for optional app analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for optional crash reporting
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring for optional app performance diagnostics
  • Google Maps or Google Play Services features where used by the app
  • Transport and weather data APIs for app functionality

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Legal Basis and User Choice

Where required, the app asks for your consent before enabling optional analytics, crash reporting, or performance data collection.

You can change privacy choices in the app settings where available. You can also control permissions such as location and notifications from your device settings.

6. Location Permission

Location permission is optional. The app requests location access only to provide location-based app functionality. If you do not grant location permission, nearby station and location-based features may not work, but other app features remain available.

7. Notification Permission

If you enable notifications, the app may send alerts about disruption updates, commute information, or travel tips. Notification permission can be changed at any time in your device settings.

8. Data Retention

Local app data, such as settings, favourites, recent stations, cached journey data, and preferences, may remain on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

Analytics, crash, diagnostic, and performance data may be retained by third-party service providers according to their retention policies.

Purchase records may be retained by Google Play, and may also be retained where required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, accounting, or refund purposes.

9. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of data associated with your app use by visiting: Data Deletion Request.

You can also delete local app data by clearing the app's storage from your device settings or uninstalling the app.

10. Children

London Tube AI is not directed at children. The app is intended for a general audience and for users who can independently use journey planning and transport information services.

11. Security

Data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit using secure connections where supported by the relevant services. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect app-related data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
